Welcome to Sovereign Arabians

We are pleased to welcome you to Sovereign Arabian Farms. We are a breeding farm for the purebred Arabian located in Jenks, Oklahoma. Our facility is conveniently located east of HWY 75, just 25 minutes south of the Tulsa Expo Equestrian Center (current location of the U.S. Arabian Nationals). Sovereign Arabians is also pleased to be home to Marc of Majik, the son of international superstar, QR Marc. Interested visitors are welcome to schedule an appointment for a visit.
