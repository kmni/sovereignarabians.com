Star Thyme LLC

This exceptional gelding was bred by Claire and Margaret Larson of Tea, South Dakota and has all of the beauty and athletic ability that his dam and sire could offer. Not only does Star possess exceptional type and motion, he compliments these qualities with extreme confidence, charisma and presence. He is the very essence of the arabian horse. Star was trained and shown by Andy Sellman (handler/trainer of Pyro Thyme) before moving on to performance training with Rob Bick. His sire has not only been shown to multiple national championships but several other championships as well. His dam is a beautiful, bay, Star Of Fame V+/ daughter who is doing very well in western pleasure (2012 US Nationals Top 10 Western Pleasure) and was also AOTH champion mare at Ohio Buckeye. Watch for him in the showring for the 2013 show season.
