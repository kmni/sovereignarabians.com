Marc of Majik

Sired by the International sensation, QR Marc, Marc of Majik represents an opportunity to breed to or purchase, in the U.S., a rare son of Res U.S. and multi World champion, QR Marc by *Marwan Al Shaqab. QR Marc was exported to Belgium by Mr. Paul Gheysens, collector of some of the finest Arabians the world has to offer. Marc of Majik is therefore not only bred for beauty through his sire line and the prepotent tail female line of his dam, but also received the charisma, style and size that his double Bey Shah dam gives.

Marc of Majik (QR Marc x Fame Miss Majik), bred by Majik Arabians, was first trained and shown with Rohara Arabians as a two year old, then, trained and shown by Shada Arabians as a 4 yr old to Scottsdale and regional showring success. Marc Of Majik's beauty and genetics, combined with his great stature, makes him an ideal cross for those who want to add size and substance to their mares without losing beauty and refinement.
