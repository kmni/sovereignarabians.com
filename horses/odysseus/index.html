KA Odysseus

KA Odysseus is sired by Odyssey SC and out of the mare Ellure A (NV Elite). With a pedigree that includes Versace, Fame VF+, the great Bey Shah+, it is not surprising that Odysseus is a Sahara Sands Junior Champion Colt, Region 10 Champion yearling sweepstakes colt and Canadian Nationals Top Ten Futurity Colt.

Odysseus is a nominated stallion in the following programs: Scottsdale Signature Stallion Auction, Minnesota Medallion Stallion Auction, Region XII Spotlight Futurity Auction, Salem Ranch Program, Colorado Breeders Cup, Western Carolinas Futurity Auction, Silver Sire Futurity, Iowa Gold Star Futurity, AHBA Futurity, and the Michigan Breeders Futurity Auction.
