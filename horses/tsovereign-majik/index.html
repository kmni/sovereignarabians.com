Tsovereign Majik

One of the last out of a direct daughter of 'The Kingmaker'. Bred by Telesha and Merlin Hochstetler of Majik Arabians, Tsovereign is close up double Bay El Bey++ and double Ivanhoe Tsultan. He is hard to miss with his shimmering coat, rich color, expressive eyes and poise. He is a tall, elegant, and beautiful athlete with a disposition that matches his name. Tsovereign is sired by Bey Shah Majik of Majik Arabians and exhibits the 'smooth elegance' of Ivanhoe Tsultan. Tsovereign made his showring debut in the Sport Horse In-Hand/Under Saddle and Dressage arena in 2012 on the breed and open circuit. The future is looking extremely promising for him in these venues. He is currently in training under saddle for the 2013 show season. Watch for this captivating stallion.

We are expecting his first foal out of Majik Kiara this spring!
