Design by Pryme SA

This very lovely grey mare has produced two beautiful foals by Exeter SMF. EXOTIC DESIGN SA trained with Greg Hazelwood and was top ten in a large international class at Scottsdale in 2012. Design's dam, DEVINE CHANCE SA, was shown by Andy Sellman and was top ten at Scottsdale in a senior mare class in 2011. Shaman Arabians was excited to be the breeder of both Tribute Thyme SA, the 2010 Canadian National Champion Stallio, and Pyro Thyme SA, the Reserve National Champion Stallion... Both sired by Pryme Thyme.
