Majik Kiara

This charming young mare is sired by Majik Jedi who is by the Multi-National Champion Stallion, Star of Fame V+/. She is out of WH Memory Lane who was bred by the world renowned breeder, Wendell Hansen and sold to Black Arabians of Sweden. WH Memory Lane is by the Bey Shah son, Beythovan MC and out of the imported Spanish sire *GG Samir who was known for passing on his beauty and talent under saddle.

We are very intrigued by this cross and expecting a beautiful, well-balanced sporthorse prospect.
